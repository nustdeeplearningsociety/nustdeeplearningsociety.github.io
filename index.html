Home

NUST Deep Learning society is a network of passionate individuals who want to share their knowledge and experience to create awareness about deep learning, an emerging field, among the masses of NUST. We aim to highlight recent research in this fascinating area and discuss possible future directions and collaborations. Our motive is to promote open research culture and a provide a platform where academic literature is discussed and valued. The society is committed to integrating deep learning study resources and conducting deep learning seminars for members as well as the general audience.
